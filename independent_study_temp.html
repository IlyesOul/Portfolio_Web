Machine Learning Independent Study 2023/2024

What it is

This is my 2023/2024 Independent Study on Machine Learning. It's partitioned into units covering different aspects of Machine Learning, where I create practical programs for real-world use

How & Why I made it

As previously mentioned, I developed the entire curriculum for this study. I presented it to my Computer Science teacher, who approved it, enabling me to dedicate a period for it during the school day. I created this study because I believed it would provide me with an excellent opportunity to explore a domain that is a personal passion and highly applicable to real word problems (and opportunities)

Curriculum

  1. Unit 1: Supervised Learning
  2. Unit 2: Unsupervised Learning
  3. Unit 3: Model Evaluation, model optimization, and data preprocessing
  4. Unit 4: Neural Networks
  5. Unit 5: Experimental/Application programs

Program Integration

Whenever I learn a new topic through the study, I either create projects that apply it, or I integrate it into an existing program. Such is the case with programs like the prioritized to-do list, Theta Terminal, Intellivest, and big ideas in the future
