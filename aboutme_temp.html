Personal meaning

there are 2 aspects of a project that give it personal meaning to me: how much it expressed my passions & curiosities, and its potential impact. my programming journey has been one of creativity, so the extent to which my projects reflect my imagination is a significant aspect of what they mean to me. the potential impact that each of my programs can have is another driver of my passion, as they give me insight into how i can use my knowledge to make impact on the real world in the future with increased access to resoruces
