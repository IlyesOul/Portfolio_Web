AI Ethics for Algerian Youth

Overview

This is an initative I created to increase the accessibility to ethical-AI use resources for Algerian youth. I collaborated with members of the Algerian community to design a curriculum that covers various topics from AI ethics and teach the course every other Saturday via Zoom around midday

Motivation

This all began with my desire to make an impact on my community using my technical passion. I initially planned on teaching an introductory-CS course at my local mosque, but I realized that there was much less attention on technology in my home country of Algeria. Figuring that it'd be more impactful (and logistcally pheasible) to teach participants non-technical concepts as opposed to programming criteria, I got the idea to focus the initative on the ethical use of AI

Impact

  1. 25 registered participants
  2. Average impact/satisfaction rating of 9/10
  3. 9 total planned lectures
  4. Potential future guest speaker events

Creation Proccess

Once I decided upon the field the course would explore, I spent weeks researching various concepts in AI ethics while designing the curriculum. Once I had a good sense of the high-level concepts I'd include, I reached out to a group of people in Algeria from a diverse variety of professions, ranging from high school teachers to PhD researchers, to understand how AI was used by Algerians in order to maximize the program's practical impact. After finalizing the course, I pitched it to various Algerian-American agencies, as well as individuals, that publicized the program
