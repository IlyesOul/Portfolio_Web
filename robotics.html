Robotics Club & Team

Overview

Throughout my time in high school, I noticed a lack in accessibility to opportunities for exploring modern fields in a practical manner. To help resolve this issue, I worked with several teachers and peers to create my school's first robotics club in years. I developed creative ways to market the club and make it as beginner friendly as possible

Club/Team

I worked with my fellow officers to develop introuctory-robotics curriculums and fun challenges for the members. I created team-based tournaments that determined our competition team, and we have, and will, competed in the regional competition Botball

Impact

  1. 20+ Club members
  2. Majority of members never had exposure to robotics
  3. 5th place in regional competition on first attempt
  4. President & Founder of club/team
